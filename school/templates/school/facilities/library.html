A Resourceful School Library

Encouraging reading habits, research skills, and lifelong learning.

The library at Peace International School serves as a vibrant learning hub where students can explore knowledge, develop reading habits, and enhance their academic growth. Our library provides a calm and welcoming environment that encourages independent learning and intellectual curiosity.

We maintain a diverse collection of books including academic textbooks, reference materials, storybooks, novels, encyclopedias, magazines, and newspapers. The library is regularly updated with new titles to support both the curriculum and students’ personal interests.

Students are encouraged to use the library for research, project work, and self-study. Comfortable reading spaces and guided library sessions help students improve comprehension, vocabulary, and critical thinking skills.

Our library also supports digital learning by providing access to educational resources, journals, and multimedia content. Teachers often integrate library-based activities into classroom assignments to promote active learning and research skills.

Through reading programmes, book fairs, storytelling sessions, and literary activities, the library plays an important role in fostering creativity, imagination, and a lifelong love for reading among students.
