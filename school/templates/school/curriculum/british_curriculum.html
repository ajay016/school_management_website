Bangladesh British Curriculum

Building strong academic foundations through the nationally approved education system.

{# Body text #}

At Peace International School, we proudly offer the Bangladesh British Curriculum, designed to provide students with a strong academic foundation, moral values, and practical life skills. The curriculum follows the guidelines set by the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB), ensuring students receive a structured and nationally recognized education.

Our British Curriculum programme emphasizes academic excellence while nurturing creativity, discipline, and social responsibility. Students are guided by experienced teachers who create engaging and supportive classroom environments that encourage active participation and critical thinking.

The curriculum includes core subjects such as Bangla, English, Mathematics, Science, Bangladesh & Global Studies, Religion, and Information & Communication Technology (ICT). We also integrate co-curricular activities, project-based learning, and modern teaching methodologies to enhance student development beyond textbooks.

Special attention is given to continuous assessment and student progress monitoring. Regular class tests, assignments, presentations, and examinations help students build confidence and prepare effectively for public examinations including SSC and HSC.

In addition to academic learning, students are encouraged to participate in cultural programmes, debates, sports, science fairs, and community engagement activities. Our goal is to develop responsible, knowledgeable, and confident individuals who are prepared for higher education and future leadership.
